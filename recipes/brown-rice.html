Brown Rice

Cooked brown rice

Ingredients:

  • One cup dry brown rice
  • Two cups water

Steps:

  1. Rinse one cup of dry brown rice with a sieve.
  2. Pour the rice into a rice cooker.
  3. Pour two cups of water into the rice cooker.
  4. Close the rice cooker and select the "Whole Grain" cook time option.
  5. Wait for rice to cook and enjoy!
