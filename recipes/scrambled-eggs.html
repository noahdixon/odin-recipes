Scrambled Eggs

Perfectly cooked and seasoned scrambled eggs

Ingredients:

  • Three large eggs (brown or white, both work)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. While the pan is heating, crack all three eggs into a bowl and whisk them with a fork.
  3. When the pan is heated, ensure that the oil is spread around the pan and pour the eggs into the pan. Immediately add salt.
  4. Frequently move the eggs around with a spatula.
  5. When the eggs are no longer soupy, removed them from the pan.
  6. Season with ground black pepper to taste.
